♟ LT Item Mail ♟

Easily send items to distant players.

Commands for everyone:

  • /itemmail - Show all commands for LT Item Mail.
  • /itemmail version - Show plugin version.
  • /sendbox - Instantly send items to other players.
  • You can also use /itemmail as /ima.
  • You can also use /sendbox as /sbx.

Commands for administrators:

  • /itemmailadmin - Administration commands for LT Item Mail.
  • /itemmailadmin version - Check for new updates.
  • You can also use /itemmailadmin as /imad.
